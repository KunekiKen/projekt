About Site

The site presents the history of basketball, from its origins and development to the present day. It tells about the key moments in the development of this sport, famous players, major competitions and the influence of basketball on world culture. Particular attention is paid to the Zalgiris team, the famous Lithuanian basketball club. It describes its history, achievements in the international arena, important games and legendary players who made Zalgiris a symbol of Lithuanian basketball.

About Basketball

Basketball is a team sport played by two teams of five players. The goal of the game is to throw the ball into the opponent's basket, located at a height of 3.05 meters. Each successful hit earns the team points: two points for a shot from close range and three for a shot from behind the three-point line. The game lasts four periods of 10 or 12 minutes, depending on the league rules. Basketball requires excellent physical fitness, coordination and teamwork. In addition to throwing the ball, players actively defend, steal the ball and perform combinations to confuse the opponent. This game is popular all over the world, especially in the United States, where the National Basketball Association (NBA) is one of the leading sports leagues. Basketball teaches discipline, strategic thinking and develops team play skills.

Zalgiris is a professional basketball club from Kaunas, Lithuania. Founded in 1944, the club has become a true symbol of Lithuanian basketball and one of the most successful in Europe. Throughout its history, Zalgiris has won the Lithuanian championship many times and has also achieved success on the international stage, including winning the Euroleague in 1999. The club is famous for its graduates, including the legendary Arvydas Sabonis, one of the greatest centers in the history of basketball. Today, Zalgiris continues to actively participate in major European tournaments and maintain a high level of Lithuanian basketball.
